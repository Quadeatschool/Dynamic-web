Overview

Purpose

This website aims to be a central hub for students to discover discounts, deals, and free services available to them through their student status. It provides clear, step-by-step instructions on how to claim these benefits—ranging from software and subscriptions to local food and transportation deals.

Audience

The site is targeted at high school, college, and university students looking to save money and make the most of their student status. It's especially helpful for incoming freshmen and those on tight budgets who may not be aware of the variety of perks they can access.

Dynamic elements

The site will use JavaScript to dynamically display benefit cards categorized by industry (Tech, Food, Subscriptions, etc.). Filters and search bars will allow students to find specific deals or types of benefits. Interactive elements like expandable details, progress steps to claim perks, and student verification status badges will enhance usability. All cards will be generated from JavaScript classes for easy scalability.

Content

Home page

The home page will introduce the Student Perk Archive and explain how the platform works. It will feature a search bar and category filter (e.g., Software, Food, Tech, Fitness, etc.). Featured perks will appear as interactive cards with company logos, discount details, and “how to claim” instructions. A short tutorial or checklist may guide new users through the claiming process.

Page 2 – Submit a Perk

Page 2 will contain a form where users can suggest new perks or benefits they know about. The form will collect the deal name, link, company, description, and optionally an image. This helps crowdsource new discounts and expand the archive over time. It may also include a short FAQ about student eligibility and verification methods.
